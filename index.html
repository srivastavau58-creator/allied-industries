About Allied Industries

Manufacturing excellence since 1998 with a commitment to precision, quality, and innovation.

Founded in 1998, Allied Industries has evolved from a small machine shop into a comprehensive manufacturing facility serving major OEMs and industrial customers across India.

Our Journey

1998: Company founded with 5 employees
2005: ISO 9001:2008 certification achieved
2015: Upgraded to ISO 9001:2015 standards
2020: Advanced CNC machining center established
2024: 50+ skilled professionals, serving 100+ clients

Our Mission

To be India's most trusted precision manufacturing partner, delivering exceptional quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction in every project we undertake.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in precision manufacturing, setting industry standards through continuous innovation, advanced technology, and sustainable practices.
